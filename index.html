Why Women Empowerment?

Just as the women of 1954 knew they had to take a stand to bring out change,it is up to us now to ensure that we create an economy that works for everyone. It is up to us to all to ensure that we create an economy that works for everyone.Women empowerment needs to become a lived reality. We dare not to be happy with the status quo which excludes many women from the social and economic mainstrem.By working together we can ensure a societal shift so that women can take their rightful place and contribute to the country's socio-economic growth and development Education remains essential and so is ensuring that girls and women have greater access to basic and higer education.There is also a pressing need to ensure that our economy is inclusive and offers women a chance at success.


Currently many women still find themselves excluded from the formal economy and in low paying jobs and economic sectors.By growing and inclusive between women and men. WE must do more to encourage women to become entrepreneurs and to start their own business.Women entrepreneurs are known to use profits from their business to improve their families living conditions amd lifestyle. More critically they also invest in their children's education which ensures that the cycle of poverty is broken.Together we have the power to overcome all our challenges and build a better tomorrow.
